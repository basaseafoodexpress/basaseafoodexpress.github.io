About Basa Seafood Express

Scott Lai emigrated to the U.S. from Vietnam over 30 years ago, and has been working in the seafood business ever since. Basa Seafood Express started in 2009, when Scott decided that he wanted to make delicious, healthy seafood affordable for anyone in the Bay Area. Through a long partnership with his suppliers, Scott always manages to get some of the best seafood available. Focusing on fresh seafood and low prices, Scott built a shining reputation among locals.

Today the shop is run by Scott’s brother Tim, who carries the same commitment to quality and value. Feel free to ask him about the best seafood of the day!
